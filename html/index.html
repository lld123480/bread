Our Mission

Let’s Get This Bread is a search engine designed to provide recipes based on individual needs, goals, and preferences. Simply enter the number of calories, time, and ingredients desired in a dish to find a large selection of customized recipes that will liven up your palate!



Why choose Let's Get This Bread?

1. Achieve Personal Nutrition Goals

With Let’s Get This Bread, everyone can achieve their nutrition and fitness goals with the easy-to-use controls to select the number of calories and ingredients the appear in the resulting recipes.

2. Find Healthy Recipes

By offering a wide range of options compiled from our grand database kitchen, healthy recipes are much more accessible and custom to all preferences. Eating healthy has just become that much easier!

3. Recipes for All Levels of Cooking Skill

Whether you are searching for a fancy dinner recipe or a quick snack, Let’s Get This Bread has a filter feature to sort recipes based on the amount of preparation time. Healthy meals can fit into anyone’s schedule!
